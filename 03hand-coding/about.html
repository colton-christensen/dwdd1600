About

Colton Christensen is a graphic designer and photographer from Holbrook, AZ. Colton graduated from Holbrook High School in 2017 and began his post-secondary eductaion at Mesa Community College in Mesa, AZ in 2018 to study graphic design.

After the COVID 19 Pandemic in 2020, Colton put his education on hold for a few years. He then moved to Utah in 2023 to resume his schooling, and is currnetly studying at Utah Valley University in Orem, UT again working towards a degree in graphic design.
